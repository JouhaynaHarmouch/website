Jouhayna Harmouch

I am Jouhayna Harmouch, a Joint-PhD student between UCA-France and UL-Lebanon. I do my research at Inria Sophia Antipolis - Méditerranée in the team AROMATH since January 2016.

I work mainly on low rank decomposition and completion methods and their applications with Bernard Mourrain and Houssam Khalil.
